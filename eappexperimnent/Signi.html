Why GreenIT is recommendable?


To Agriculture, whether one is a novice or a seasoned educator, they should consider implementing various strategies to boost student motivation and inspire them to reach their full potential. Even the most well-meaning and knowledgeable educators occasionally struggle to keep their students on track. As students that seek for learnings, they will be derived on thirst to every aspect of knowledge to apply in their life. It will sustain their critical thinking and develop skills that will help them enhance on their own.

To Society, they will benefit from GreenIT as this is focus to lessen the usage of fossil fuels that contributes to greenhouses and they will also acquire more knowledge and awareness about what's been happening not just to our surroundings but also to the things they used in everyday life. They can also teach students how they can save up on using electricity so it won't cause much time and cost and to be able to help their family by avoiding e-waste. Incorporate practical environmental projects within the educational program. Get students involved in local conservation initiatives, garbage audits, or energy

To Agriculture, the GreenIT will influence them to proper cultivation of crops. By not letting it affect the environment, both nature and animals won't be in harm because of recycling. GreenIT will encourage farmers to help in balancing eco system. It will also enhance the strength of supply chain and by that, more goods will be produced as it will also benefit the economic development.
