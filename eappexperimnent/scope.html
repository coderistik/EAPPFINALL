What is the Scope of this Study?

The scope of this study is to Grade 12 students to encourage them to use eco-friendly technology to minimize our pollution. This study aims to inform and to enhance the knowledge of Grade 12 students in order to implement by schools or in our community. In our generation, e-waste is the one of the major problem we are trying to figure out on how it will minimize the production of it. This study also recommends the alternative way instead of using technology that contributes pollution specifically e-waste. It will be helpful for Grade 12 in their study, health and community that is why, it is the scope of our study.


Target Audience

The target audience for Green Information Technology is students because with the rising change in our climate, we will need the students to help fight and address climate change in the future. Also people who work in an IT profession would benefit in this because, people who work in this industry could influence in promoting environmentally friendly electronics products that may help the planet reducing electronic waste. Government agencies can also benefit from green IT, because implementing green IT may help reduce energy consumption that would benefit the world. Lastly the global population can benefit from green IT because this will help to reduce the carbon footprint and address climate change.
